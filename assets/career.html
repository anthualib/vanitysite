A study estimates that 90 percent of students in universities experience issues due to academic stress, future planning, mental health. However, only 15 percent of students reported actually seeking help. As a longtime information specialist and aspiring librarian, I aim to make finding support more accessible. I have a core philosophy that small but thoughtful actions can make biggest changes. Though my work, I hope to manifest this by being invested and present for those seeking guidance.
